# Proposal

---

- What problem does your app solve?
Keeping track of transactions over a trip and splitting it evenly between people

- Be as specific as possible; how does your app solve the problem?
You can keep track of events that happen within the trip
Description of event/date
Keep track of all expenses
List the people who are involved
Track who has paid their share (I think)
Totaling cost at the end of the trip
Making it easy for groups to split trip costs

- What is the mission statement?
“Equal contributions by everyone”

# Features

---

- What features are required for your minimum viable product?
Onboarding: login/signup
Create trip
Number of people/Names of people
Destination
Date
Edit/Delete option
View all events, current/completed
Trip expense item title
- What features may you wish to put in a future release?
Ability to split custom amounts
Add friends and view their trips (allow user to show/hide expenses)
Connect people involved in trip to actual registered users

- What do the top 3 similar apps do for their users?
Tracking expenses
History of payments
Tracking your trip (Road Trippers)
